my releases

Bedroom pop, during pandemic My lifelong commitment to music as my primary form of self-expression, love language, and sanctuary has so far brought about my emergence as a published artist under the name Astrë. During quarantine I released my first song "Moon," and later followed that up with my first album "Genesis" in 2021. Have a listen!

It's crazy for me to think about my younger years and the little melodies I'd write and record on my Leap Pad when I should've been sleeping. I'm glad that my years as an avid music enthusiast and performer has finally brought me to a place in which I'm ready to share my passion with the world. For now, my goal moving foward is to reconnect with my original instrument (the cello) and write some new songs with it!
