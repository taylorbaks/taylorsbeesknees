my releases - astrë

During quarantine in 2020, I wrote, produced, and published my song Moon on all platforms. I published the song under my artist name Astrë, which means star in Latin and French, but also hearth and home in the english language. I loved learning about this word in my earlier Latin classes, so I just ran with it! I later published my first EP, entitled “Genesis,” in 2021.

about my music history

Music has played one of the most important roles in my life in shaping who I’ve become. Even in the womb, I would kick my feet when I heard opera or dance music. Then, as a child, I memorized Beyonce and Michael Jackson and found accidental harmonies. All the way until now, when I can barely go a single day without creating a new Spotify playlist or writing a song.

my instruments

Writing and performing music, in particular, have always been my greatest forms of self-expression. I began in my early youth by writing song lyrics in my bed when I was meant to be asleep, to pass the time. I would hum and sing melodies and record them on my LeapPad to show my parents the next day.


The first instrument that I formally learned to play was the cello. I loved to teach myself Ed Sheeran songs and also try my hand at composing. Then, I taught myself how to play the guitar during quarantine due to an abundance of time. I also bought my first Midi board during this time and began to play around with drum patterns and digitally produced sounds.

recent tunes

My first film camera was a gift from an old friend who was moving down South. Handling a slow, finicky AE-1 was absolutely a steep learning curve, but I loved having to put intention into the images I captured. Now, film photography holds a special place in my heart, much like music. It's a way to feel, to fully experience life, and to invite others to join in on my journey. To me, film photography is about the events behind the picture and rarely about the end result. It's about the painstaking ISO adjustments, the clammy palms as you approach the end of a roll, and the breath-holding moments when you know you've captured the perfect photograph through the viewfinder. I hope you've enjoyed some of my photography; a little window into my view of the world!

oshun

power is a woman
and though men have captured her
im saving her now
im marching through mimosa fields
im leading my sisters as Venus rules the sky
